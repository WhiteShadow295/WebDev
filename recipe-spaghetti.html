Ingredients

Things to prepare:

  1. Spaghetti
  2. Pasta sauce
  3. Tomato
  4. Potato
  5. salt and pepper
  6. Onion
  7. Minced meat(chicken, beef, pork)

Fisrt step

Cook the spaghetti with boiling water

Step 2

Put some oil and heat them

Step 3

Put some minced meat and fry them until half cook

Step 4

Add some onion then fry it until minced meat are fully cooked.

Step 5

When the minced meat are fully cook, add some tomato
and add some salt and pepper

Step 6

add some potato(ps: can let potato soak in water
before put them into pan so the potato will be fully cook.)

Step 7

add pasta sauce and let it cook 15-25 minutes

Final

At the end, serve the pasta sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Ready to serve.Enjoy
