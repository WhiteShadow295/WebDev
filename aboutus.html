About Us

88Freshmart is one of the best mart in Malaysia and it supplies daily items such as meats, fishes, fruits and vegetables. 88Freshmart was founded in 2003 and started its operation in Terengganu by a businessman in order to help the struggling retailer ,farmers and fishers to sell their fresh products .The businessman has several networks with factories to supply fresh products to sell. 88Freshmart is known for its freshness of products .88 Freshmart has a vision to give the farmers and fishers a better life by helping them sell their gains from all-day work


88Freshmart has the best technology in Malaysia in order to keep the products fresh during the delivery process. 88 Freshmart has a few delivery partners such as JnT and GDEx. They also provide free delivery door-to-door to our customers.88 Freshmart is well aware of the contemporary and near future needs, hence, strives to meet both expectations resulting in a vast variety of the latest groceries products with affordable prices.


As the business went well, 88Freshmart had started to expand their business into several branches.Today, 88 Freshmart had expanded their business in several states, notably Selangor , Kuala Lumpur, Perak and Pahang. Each state has a few branches ,KL has the most branches ,which is 6 and the store in Selangor has 5 branches meanwhile in Perak has 4 while Pahang has 4 .The main shop is located at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
